Privacy Policy — AFT Pro

This policy explains what data AFT Pro collects, how it is used, and what controls users have.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account and authentication data (if you sign in): cloud user ID, email for email/password sign-in, and basic sign-in metadata from Apple or Google.
  • Fitness test data you enter: AFT inputs, calculated scores, saved score sets, timestamps, and profile defaults.
  • Support submissions: support, bug, or feature messages with metadata such as user ID, app version, build number, platform, and timestamp.
  • Operational diagnostics: when profile sync fails, we may log error details (error code, short message, operation, user ID, platform, and timestamp) to improve reliability.
  • Guest mode: data may be stored only on-device until you sign in.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide core app functionality (scoring, standards, saved sets, and exports).
  • Maintain sign-in and account access when you choose to authenticate.
  • Sync your profile and saved data across devices while signed in.
  • Review support requests and troubleshoot reliability issues.

3. What We Do Not Use

  • We do not use Google Analytics.
  • We do not use Google Crashlytics.

4. Data Storage and Sharing

  • Some data is stored on-device (including guest-mode data).
  • Signed-in data is stored in cloud services used by the app.
  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We use service providers for authentication and cloud storage. User-initiated exports are shared only when you choose recipients through device sharing tools.

5. Your Controls

  • You can use guest mode without a full account.
  • You can sign out at any time.
  • You can delete your account, saved score sets, and associated cloud-synced profile data inside the app: Settings → Account → Delete account.

6. Security and Retention

  • We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards.
  • Data in transit between app and backend services is generally encrypted.
  • We retain data as needed to provide app functionality, subject to deletion requests and operational or legal requirements.

7. Contact

For privacy requests or questions, email privacy@nunntechnologies.com. You can also use the in-app support options. This policy may be updated periodically, and the date above will be revised when changes are made.
